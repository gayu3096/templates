Volume analysis from laser scan

Overview

WildplantTS digital volume Application calculates the Volume of the enclosed object that can be of any scans. In general, Volume is a way we measure the unit of three-dimensional space of an enclosed body. We use laser scan data as the primary resource and create an enclosed mesh model since point clouds will be not in the form of a surface or body. We begin this process by polygonising the triangular skin between every three points and making it a surface. Once the entire point cloud is converted into 3D mesh model software that calculates the Volume of the enclosed mesh model, this Volume can be calculated from the plane or anything. Combined with our 3D laser scanning and digital volume calculation application, we can 3D scan an object, building pits or anything that demands volume
